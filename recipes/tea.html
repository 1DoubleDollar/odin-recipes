Tea

Ingredients

  • 100ml milk
  • 100ml water
  • Sugar
  • Ginger paste
  • 5g tea powder

Steps

  1. boil 100ml water
  2. Add sugar (according to taste) in boiling water
  3. Add tea leaves (powder)
  4. Add Ginger paste
  5. When on boil come add 100ml milk in it
  6. boil it until it color become darker
